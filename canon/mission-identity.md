## Mission
Still Point exists to make the breath easier to feel, and therefore easier to return to—especially when attention wanders. It amplifies the felt signal of breathing at the nostrils, supporting calm, clarity, and sustained concentration through direct sensation rather than effort.

## Identity
Still Point is a purpose-built **breath anchor**: worn externally and self-retaining by contour fit. It anchors on the outer nasal sidewalls while floating around the nasal tip, preserving full airflow while expanding the sensory field around the nostrils. The device is designed to “disappear” into practice—quiet, gentle, and reliable—so the user is left with what matters: breath, body, presence.

## One-sentence definition
A small, soft, nose-worn meditation tool that rests on the outside of the nose, never enters the nostrils, and increases breath vividness by framing airflow and amplifying sensation around the nostrils—especially the lower region.

## The promise
- The breath becomes easier to locate.
- Attention returns more quickly, more often.
- The nervous system settles without forcing.
- Practice feels more embodied, less mental.

## Values (design + philosophy)
- **Simplicity over complexity:** no tech, no tracking, no performance pressure.
- **Gentleness over force:** resting contact, not clamping; supportive, not corrective.
- **Embodiment over ideas:** sensation leads; the mind follows.
- **Reliability over novelty:** a tool you can trust, anywhere, anytime.

## Brand voice
Quiet. Clear. Warm. Minimal. Non-performative. Calm confidence without hype.
