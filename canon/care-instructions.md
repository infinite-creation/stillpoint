### Caring for Your StillPoint

**Everyday cleaning**
Rinse gently with warm water after each use. For a deeper clean, apply a small drop of mild dish soap, work it in gently with your fingertips, then rinse thoroughly under warm running water — take a moment to make sure all soap is fully cleared. Let it air dry completely before storing or wearing again.

**Disinfecting**
If you want to disinfect, dampen a soft lint-free cloth with 60–70% isopropyl alcohol and wipe the surface gently. Allow to air dry fully before use. Do not spray sanitizer or alcohol directly onto the device.

**What to avoid**
Bleach, hydrogen peroxide, vinegar, ethanol-based hand sanitizers, and abrasive cloths or brushes can all damage TPU — causing yellowing, surface tackiness, or cloudiness. Do not soak the device in any liquid.

**Storage**
Keep in a clean, dry place away from direct heat and prolonged sunlight when not in use.

## Material Notes (internal reference)
- Material: TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) — soft, flexible, skin-contact safe
- 60–70% isopropyl alcohol is safe for occasional disinfecting; ethanol-based sanitizers are not
- Dish soap leaves residue if not rinsed thoroughly — the rinsing step is critical for a device worn at the nose
- TPU is vulnerable to strong solvents, prolonged moisture, and high pH; avoid all three
- Do not put in dishwasher
