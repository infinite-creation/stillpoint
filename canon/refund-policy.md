# Returns & Refunds Policy — StillPoint

**Version:** v1.0
**Last updated:** March 14, 2026
**Status:** Approved — live on site and in Shopify

---

We want you to love your StillPoint. If it isn't the right fit, we'll make it right.

**How to start a return**

Contact us at hello@trystillpoint.life with your order number and we'll take care of you from there.

**Our return process**

Because StillPoint is a personal care product worn on the nose, returned devices can't be restocked or resold. Rather than ask you to package and ship it back, we keep things simple: once your return is approved, we'll ask you to cut your StillPoint into four pieces with household scissors, snap a quick photo, and email it to us. Once we receive your photo, we'll process your full refund — no return shipping required.

**Refunds**

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 10 business days of confirmation. If more than 15 business days have passed, reach out at hello@trystillpoint.life.

**Damaged or incorrect items**

Please inspect your order when it arrives. If your StillPoint is defective, damaged, or incorrect, contact us at hello@trystillpoint.life and we'll make it right immediately.

**Exchanges**

To exchange for a different size, contact us to start the process and we'll guide you through it.

**Sale items and gift cards**

We're unable to offer refunds on sale items or gift cards.

**European Union customers**

If your order ships to the EU, you have the right to cancel or return within 14 days of receipt under EU consumer protection law. Please contact us at hello@trystillpoint.life to initiate.
