## 6) FAQ (friction killers)

### Q: Does it block breathing?
No. Still Point is worn externally and is designed to leave airflow unobstructed.

### Q: Does it go inside the nostrils?
No. It never enters the nostrils.

### Q: Is it a nasal dilator?
No. Still Point is not designed to open the airway or change breathing. It’s a breath-awareness tool.

### Q: Will it fit my nose?
Still Point is designed to rest securely by contour fit on the outer nasal sidewalls. (If you later offer sizes: add sizing guidance here.)

### Q: How do I wear it?
Place it gently on the nose so it rests on the outer sidewalls. It should feel stable and comfortable, not pinching.

### Q: Can I talk while wearing it?
Yes. It’s designed to be unobtrusive and to leave the mouth and lip movement free.

### Q: How long do I wear it?
As long as it feels comfortable—many people use it for a full meditation session or during wind-down.

### Q: Can I use it for sleep?
Many people use it at bedtime as a breath anchor. Comfort varies by person. Use what feels safe and comfortable for your body.

### Q: How do I clean it?
Wash with mild soap and warm water, rinse well, and let it fully dry.

### Q: Is this a medical device?
No. Still Point is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
