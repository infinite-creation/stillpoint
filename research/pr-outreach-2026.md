## The Gift Package

Keep it minimal and brand-aligned. The packaging experience should feel like StillPoint.

**Suggested contents:**
- StillPoint device (one unit — Small or Medium)
- A short, handwritten note from Michael (founder story in 3–4 sentences)
- A printed product card (see press kit template below)
- Optional: a small card with the "The breath doesn't change in volume — it changes in vividness." quote

**Package feel:** Clean, simple, no clutter. Kraft paper, minimal printing, cream or white palette. Let the product speak.

## Pitch Email Template

> ⚠️ **Important:** Personalize each email before sending. Reference something specific from the editor's recent work. Editors receive hundreds of pitches — a sentence that shows you've read their work dramatically increases response rate. All copy should sound like Michael, not a press release.

---

**Subject line options (choose one per editor — test different):**
- `Something new: the first nose-worn breath anchor for meditation`
- `A tiny tool that makes the breath easier to feel — wanted to share`
- `New product for meditators — no app, no battery, just breath`

---

**Pitch email (template — personalize the opening):**

---

Hi [Editor name],

I've been following your work at [Publication] — [one specific sentence referencing a recent piece, topic, or angle they've covered]. It's part of what made me want to reach out to you specifically.

I'm the founder of StillPoint, and I wanted to share something I think your readers would genuinely find interesting.

StillPoint is a small, external breath anchor worn on the nose. There's nothing else quite like it — it doesn't go inside the nostrils, it doesn't change how much you breathe, and it requires no app, no battery, and no setup. It sits gently on the outer nasal sidewalls and redirects airflow so that the breath becomes more vivid — cooler on the inhale, warmer on the exhale. The result is that meditation becomes easier, because the breath is easier to feel and return to.

I designed it because I kept seeing the same challenge: the breath is the most common anchor in meditation, but it can feel subtle — especially when the mind is busy. StillPoint amplifies that sensation without changing anything about how you breathe.

It's handcrafted in the USA. First Batch of 400 ships May 2026.

I'd love to send you one to try. No obligation — I just think it's the kind of thing that's easier to understand once you've experienced it. Happy to answer any questions or share more about the mechanism if it's useful for a story.

Thank you for the work you do,

Michael Usowski

## Notes & Reminders

- **Breathe Magazine** explicitly says no AI-generated submissions. Write that pitch entirely by hand.
- **Well+Good** loves samples — their commerce team often passes products among multiple editors. Send 2 units if possible.
- **Mindful Magazine** is the dream placement. Be patient — they're selective. A personal, story-driven pitch will land better than a product pitch.
- **Affiliate programs** — several editors noted that having an affiliate/commission link helps get products included in digital roundups. Worth setting up a simple affiliate program through Shopify before the September pitch window.
- Always send packages to arrive **Tuesday–Thursday** — Monday packages get buried, Friday ones get forgotten over the weekend.
