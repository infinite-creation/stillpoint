# Prompt: Ad → Checkout Journey Simulator — Still Point

## Purpose
Simulate the psychological, emotional, and cognitive journey of a potential customer from first exposure to an ad through checkout decision.

This tool helps identify:

- Trust formation gaps
- Confusion points
- Emotional drop-off zones
- Messaging discontinuities
- Conversion friction
- Archetype resonance mismatches

The goal is to refine creative and landing alignment before deploying ad spend.

## Input

Accept one or more of:

- Ad concept
- Reel script
- Hook + caption
- Landing page section
- Full campaign description

---

## Simulation Instructions

Model the journey for:

### 3 Archetypes Minimum
Select the most relevant archetypes from profiles.

Simulate their internal experience across stages.

Avoid generic marketing language — represent realistic thought patterns.

---

## Journey Stages

### 1️⃣ Scroll Encounter
Evaluate:

- Attention capture probability
- Curiosity activation
- Emotional tone reception
- Initial skepticism

Output:
- Likely thought patterns
- Engagement probability (Low / Medium / High)

---

### 2️⃣ Ad Consumption
Evaluate:

- Clarity of understanding
- Trust signals
- Confusion points
- Sensory imagination activation

Output:
- Emotional trajectory
- Key persuasion drivers
- Points of doubt

---

### 3️⃣ Click Decision
Assess:

- Motivation to click
- Hesitation triggers
- Curiosity vs resistance balance

Output:
- Click likelihood rating
- Dominant decision factor

---

### 4️⃣ Landing Page Experience
Simulate:

- Expectation validation
- Narrative continuity
- Trust reinforcement
- Cognitive overload risk
- Product comprehension depth

Output:
- Engagement depth estimate
- Emotional alignment score
- Friction zones

---

### 5️⃣ Purchase Decision Moment
Model:

- Price-value reflection
- Self-justification reasoning
- Identity resonance
- Risk perception

Output:
- Conversion likelihood
- Objection list
- Needed reassurance

---

## Final Output Summary

### Conversion Risk Map
Identify:

- Highest drop-off stage
- Psychological bottlenecks
- Messaging mismatches

---

### Optimization Opportunities
Provide actionable recommendations for:

- Hook adjustment
- Script refinement
- Visual sequencing
- Landing edits
- Trust signal placement

Prioritize by impact.

---

### Confidence Score
Rate overall journey strength:

- Fragile
- Moderate
- Strong
- High Conversion Potential

Explain reasoning.
