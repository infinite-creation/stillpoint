# Prompt: Landing Page Alignment Check — Still Point

## Purpose
Evaluate whether ad creative concepts, messaging, or hooks align with the Still Point landing page experience and support conversion rather than creating cognitive dissonance.

This prevents:
- Drop-off from expectation mismatch
- Trust erosion
- Confusion about product function
- Overclaim framing

## Instructions

Given:
- Ad script
- Hook
- Messaging angle
- Visual narrative
- Or full campaign concept

Perform a structured alignment analysis.

---

## Output Structure

### 1️⃣ Message Match
Evaluate:
- Does the promise match what the landing page delivers?
- Is framing consistent with site positioning?
- Any emotional tone mismatch?

Score:
- Strong Match
- Partial Match
- Misaligned

Explain reasoning.

---

### 2️⃣ Expectation Gap Detection
Identify:
- Claims stronger than site support
- Benefits implied but not clarified
- Sensory experience mismatches
- Audience targeting drift

List specific gaps.

---

### 3️⃣ Conversion Friction Prediction
Predict potential user hesitation caused by:

- Confusion about use
- Novelty discomfort
- Trust questions
- Credibility gaps
- Missing reassurance

Rank friction risk:
- Low
- Moderate
- High

---

### 4️⃣ Landing Page Reinforcement Suggestions
Recommend adjustments to:

- Hook wording
- Script emphasis
- Visual sequencing
- CTA framing
- Demonstration clarity

Focus on improving continuity.

---

### 5️⃣ Archetype Alignment Check
Identify:
- Target archetype(s)
- Whether landing page speaks to them effectively
- Where resonance breaks down

Suggest refinement if needed.

---

### 6️⃣ Claims Safety Verification
Confirm compliance with:

- Guardrails
- Non-medical positioning
- Experiential framing

Flag risks explicitly.
