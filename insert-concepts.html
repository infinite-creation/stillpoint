StillPoint

Laser-cut cardstock shipping insert concepts

01 — Silhouette Cradle
Single flat sheet. A U-shaped cutout mirrors the product form — StillPoint slots in from above and is held by friction.
02 — Folded Tray
Scored and folded insert with raised walls. Product nestles in a central recess, surrounded by low walls that prevent shifting.
03 — Floating Island
Two layers glued together. The top layer has a product silhouette cutout, creating a recessed "island." Feels premium on unboxing.
04 — Fold-Over Wrap
A single scored strip folds over the product. Die-cut locking slots on each end click together — no box required, or sits inside one.

All concepts shown in flat/plan view. Green outline = StillPoint product. Dashed lines = score/fold lines. Darker areas = laser cut zones.
