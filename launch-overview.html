Shipping note: USPS First-Class Mail for envelopes handles up to 3.5 oz. If Still Point (device + any insert) comes in under that, you're in ideal territory — typically $0.68–$1.35 per piece. Above 3.5 oz it shifts to First-Class Package (up to 13 oz), which runs $4–$6. Worth weighing the device + envelope before committing. Even a simple folded card inside the envelope can elevate the unboxing feel without adding much weight.
