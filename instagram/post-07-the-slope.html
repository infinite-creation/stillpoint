Some sits are clear, still, deeply concentrated. Others are scattered, restless, relentlessly busy. Both are completely normal. The practice isn't about having a perfect sit. It's about showing up again, sit after sit, and watching the direction you're moving over time. The slope of the curve is what matters — not any single session. StillPoint doesn't promise transcendence. It makes each sit a little easier to begin, a little easier to stay with, a little clearer to return to.
