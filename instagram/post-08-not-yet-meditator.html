Your mind is shaping your life. Every choice, every reaction, every feeling that lingers a little too long — it all begins in the mind. Most of us go our whole lives without really looking at that. Without understanding what's actually running the show. Meditation is the practice of looking. Not to fix anything, not to become someone different — just to see what's here. The breath is the simplest door in. It's always present. It doesn't require belief or training to begin — just attention.
